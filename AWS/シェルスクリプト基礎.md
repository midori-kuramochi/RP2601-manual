### for文（繰り返し文）
#!/bin/sh

for KAWAIILAB in fruitzipper candycune sweetstedy cutiestreet morestar
do
        touch "$KAWAIILAB"

done

流れイメージ
- KAWAIILABという変数を使ってfor（繰り返し処理）をするんだ！
- 何回繰り返す？→値の数だけ
- 1. 変数　KAWAIILAB=fruitzipper
     touch fruitzipper
- 2. 変数　KAWAIILAB=candycune
     touch candycune
- 3. 変数　KAWAIILAB=sweetstedy
     touch sweetstedy
- 4. 変数　KAWAIILAB=cutiestreet
     touch cutiestreet
- 5. 変数　KAWAIILAB=morestar
     touch morestar
